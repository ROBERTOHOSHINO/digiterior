KANRI White Paper 2026

Knowledge & Resource Intelligence Platform

From Resource Management to Agentic Management

Executive Summary

KANRI is a next-generation platform designed to organize, connect, and activate enterprise knowledge.

Unlike traditional CRM, ERP, or workflow systems, KANRI focuses on four core enterprise resources:

  • People
  • Products & Services
  • Time
  • Value

By integrating these resources through Master Data Management, Learning Loops, and AI Agents, KANRI transforms operational records into organizational intelligence.

Chapter 1 – Origins of KANRI

1991 – Database Thinking

The journey began with a simple question: How can organizations retain knowledge beyond individual employees?

2006 – Dynamics CRM

The introduction of Microsoft Dynamics CRM revealed that customer relationships, projects, tasks, and business processes could be connected in a unified platform.

2016 – BazaarBazaar

The BazaarBazaar concept expanded the vision beyond CRM into communities, commerce, projects, suppliers, and customers.

2026 – Agent Era

AI Agents finally enable accumulated business knowledge to become active and operational.
